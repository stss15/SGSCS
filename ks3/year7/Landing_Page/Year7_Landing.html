Term 1

Introduction to Computer Science

What is Computer Science? Explore the core ideas, its impact on the world, and the exciting journey ahead in KS3.
Term 2

E-Safety

Explore the digital realm & become a Cyber Hero! Learn online safety, digital footprints, and how to outsmart digital dangers.
Term 3

Computational Thinking

Think like a coder! Unravel tricky problems by breaking them down, spotting patterns, and designing clever, logical solutions.
Term 4

Programming Techniques

Command the computer! Learn essential techniques like sequence, selection (if/else), and iteration (loops) to write clear instructions.
Term 5

Computer Systems

Look under the hood! Discover the amazing teamwork between hardware (parts you touch) and software (programs) that brings tech to life.
Term 6

Data Representation

Crack the code! How do computers store everything using only 0s and 1s? Decode the secret language of binary data.
